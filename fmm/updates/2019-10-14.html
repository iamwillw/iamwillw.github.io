Alcove, pumpkin tree, and last section of front yard fencing

Posted: October 14, 2019

Today's main focus was on the alcove. We:

  • hung pumpkins in the tree.
  • placed some old fashioned lighted witches, ghosts, Frankenstein monsters, and mummies. They follow along the sidewalk inside the fence. They were a great find a garage sale a few years back.
  • setup 3 of the witches around a cauldron and in front of the "Witch is in" sign as the fence meets the porch.
  • posted a new "Trick or Treat" sign on the other side of the walkway.
  • constructed the last pieces of fencing we can for now. We add the final 2 pieces of fence on Halloween day.

Finally, the last few photos are as it's getting dark so you can see how some of the lights look.
