Club Dead and cemetery detail work

Posted: October 12, 2019

Today the weather cooperated again. We finished the detail work on the band stage area of Club Dead. This included power and lights as well the setup of Fright Night's backup band members.

We mounted the lights on the front of the mausoleum and hung the cemetery sign.

Finally, we assembled the front porch Witches Parlor walls and setup the fireplace. This is where our trick or treaters get their candy on Halloween night.
