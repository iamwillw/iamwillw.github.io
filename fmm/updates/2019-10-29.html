The pumpkin patch comes alive

Posted: October 29, 2019

On Sunday, October 27, we setup the basic layout of our pumpkin patch. There are a handful of lighted pumpkins in the patch. 2 bumpkins watch over it and have lighted pumpkin heads. We also decorated a couple of bushes with our candy corn lights. We're getting close to the big day and this is the last of the outside work we do ahead of time.
