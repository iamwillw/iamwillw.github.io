Updates

Club Dead and cemetery are almost complete!

Posted: October 26, 2019

This update covers the last couple of weeks.

For the weekend of October 19-20, we:

  • completed some minor prop work.
  • planned for the next weekend.
  • wrote out our Halloween day list of activities.
  • did some clean-up in the shop.

On October 24-25, I built a new box that holds the monitor, a light, and a fan for the Fright Night show. Along with the logo on the front, we also placed 3 skulls and will have a couple of battery operated candles.

Saturday, October 26, we finished up as much of Club Dead and the cemetery as we can before Halloween. There are a handful of props that we only put on out Halloween day. Otherwise, both the club and cemetery are complete. We also:

  • hung some mummy faces to the wall where the Angel of Death sits.
  • added an LED light strip across the front of the club that changes to the beat of the music. We also added some lights around the Club Dead sign (in the last photo below). After seeing them in the dark, we decided to remove them and have a single red light shine on the letters.
  • did a full light test to make sure that everything was working and lit up as we wanted.
  • tested the new fog pipe now that it's buried in the ground.
  • ran through a full audio check to set our initial levels and make sure that all our connections were good.
