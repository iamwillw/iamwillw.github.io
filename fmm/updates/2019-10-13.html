Mausoleum finished, new fog pipe, and tombstone lighting

Posted: October 13, 2019

We focused on work in the cemetery. First we completed the detail work in the mausoleum. Along with hanging the Ghost and placing the coffin inside, we also placed our Zombie Owl to keep watch. Then, we placed and tested the lights for the tombstones. Our final project was the new "bendy" fog pipe. There were a few raindrops, but otherwise it was another great weather day!
